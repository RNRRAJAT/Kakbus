ii) LM339 IC -1
iii)16*2 LCD WITH I2C - 1  
iv) LM7805-1
v)100nF Capacitors -2
vi)22pF   Capacitors- 2,16 MHz -1
vii)1N4007 -2
viii)10K Resistors- 5 
ix)220 ohm,150 ohm Resistor-1
x)330 ohm rsistors -2
xi)PUSH BUTTONS - 3
xii)Slide Switch -1,LED-1
It can measure precisely ->
i)Inductance -> from (10uH-1H)
ii)Small Capacitances -> from ( 3 pF – 100 nF)
iii)Big Capacitances -> from (100 nF – 5mF)
